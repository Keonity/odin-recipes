Five Ingredient Tomato Soup

Description

Simple recipe for delicious five-ingredient artisan's tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 28oz cans of San Marzano tomatoes (whole)
  • 1 stick of salted butter
  • 1 yellow onion, peel removed and quartered
  • 3-4 cloves smashed garlic
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Simmer tomatoes, butter, and onion in large saucepan at medium low for 45 minutes. Cover partially with a lid. Stir occasionally.
  2. Remove onion pieces using tongs.
  3. Blend until smooth as desired with immersion blender
  4. Enjoy! Top with pesto if desired.
