Sorbet

Description

This simple 2-ingredient recipe is highly customizable and creates a fruity and frosty treat!

Ingredients

  • Frozen fruit of choice
  • Coconut milk

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a fruit processer and blend until smooth.
  2. Store in freezer until ready to eat.
  3. Thaw for a couple of minutes after taking out of freezer.
  4. Enjoy!
